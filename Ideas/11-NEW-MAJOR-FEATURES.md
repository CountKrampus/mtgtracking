# New Major Feature Ideas

## High Priority

### Collection Intelligence
- **Rules Engine / Judge Tool**: Look up comprehensive rules, keyword definitions, and card rulings from Scryfall
- **Combo Database Browser**: Browse Commander Spellbook combos beyond just what's in your collection
- **Format Legality Checker**: Check if a specific card is legal in any format with ban history
- **Reprint Tracker**: Track upcoming reprints that may affect card values (using spoiler season data)
- **Set Release Calendar**: Display upcoming MTG set releases with prerelease dates and card count
- **Spoiler Season Integration**: View and track newly spoiled cards from upcoming sets

### Advanced Deck Tools
- **Deck Playtester**: Full solitaire goldfish mode — draw hands, play lands, cast spells, track mana
- **Match Tracker**: Log competitive match results (2-1, 0-2) with sideboard changes
- **Tournament Report Writer**: Structured tournament report template with match results
- **Deck Archetype Recognition**: Auto-detect deck archetype from card list (Aggro, Midrange, Control, Combo)
- **Metagame Dashboard**: Track local meta based on decks played in your group
- **Limited (Draft/Sealed) Pool Builder**: Import a limited pool and get deck-building suggestions

### Collection Tools
- **Insurance Mode**: Track replacement cost, take photos of expensive cards, generate insurance documentation
- **Binder Organizer**: Visual binder page layout — drag cards into 3x3 or 4x3 binder page slots
- **Collection Goals**: Set and track goals ("Complete Dominaria", "Own all swords cycle", "100 Mythics")
- **Card Condition Tracker**: Photo-based condition tracking with before/after comparisons over time
- **Lending Library**: Track cards lent to friends with due dates and reminders

## Medium Priority

### Gameplay Tools
- **Planechase Mode**: Random plane selection with planar die integration
- **Archenemy Mode**: Scheme deck support for Archenemy format
- **Kingdoms Variant**: Role assignment (King, Knight, Bandit, etc.) for multiplayer variant
- **Star Variant**: Seating and enemy/ally tracking for Star format
- **Custom Format Builder**: Define custom format rules (card pool, ban list, special rules)
- **Cube Builder**: Build and manage draft cubes with card list and draft tracking
- **Sealed Simulator**: Open virtual booster packs from any set and build a sealed deck

### Discovery & Learning
- **Card Rulings Browser**: View official rulings for any card (from Scryfall rulings data)
- **Interaction Checker**: "How do these two cards interact?" — rules lookup for common interactions
- **New Player Guide**: Built-in guide explaining MTG basics, card types, phases, keywords
- **Keyword Glossary**: Searchable glossary of all MTG keywords with explanations
- **Combo Tutorials**: Step-by-step explanations of popular combos
- **Format Guides**: Overview of each format (Standard, Modern, Commander, etc.) with what makes them unique

### Creative Tools
- **Custom Card Creator**: Design custom/proxy cards with your own art and text
- **Alter Art Gallery**: Upload photos of card alters and link to the card entry
- **Token Generator**: Create custom token cards with uploaded art
- **Playmat Designer**: Design custom playmats using your card art collection
- **Deck Box Label Maker**: Generate labels for deck boxes with deck name, colors, and stats

## Lower Priority

### Integration & Ecosystem
- **EDHREC Full Integration**: Pull card recommendations, themes, and combos from EDHREC
- **MTG Goldfish Integration**: Price data and metagame information from MTG Goldfish
- **TappedOut Integration**: Import/export with TappedOut
- **Arena Deck Sync**: Keep Arena and paper decks in sync

### Fun & Novelty
- **Card Art Gallery**: Full-screen browsable gallery of card art from your collection
- **Art Quiz Game**: "Which card is this art from?" quiz using your collection
- **Collection Bingo**: Randomly generated bingo cards based on collection goals
- **Time Capsule**: Lock a snapshot of your collection and open it in a year to see changes
- **"What Should I Play?" Randomizer**: Random deck selector weighted by recent play frequency
- **Card Trivia**: Random MTG trivia questions about cards in your collection
- **Flavor Text of the Day**: Daily random flavor text from your collection
- **Collection NFT (just kidding)**: Export your collection as a cool shareable digital card
