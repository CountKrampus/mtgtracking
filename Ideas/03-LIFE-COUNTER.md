# Life Counter Ideas

## High Priority

### Gameplay Enhancements
- **Poison / Infect Counter**: Track poison counters per player (lose at 10); separate from life total
- **Energy Counter**: Track energy counters (Kaladesh mechanic)
- **Experience Counter**: Track experience counters (Commander 2015+)
- **Radiation Counter**: Track rad counters (Fallout set)
- **The Ring Tempts You Tracker**: Track ring-bearer and temptation level (LotR set)
- **Monarch Tracker**: Visual crown indicator for the current monarch; auto-remind draw step
- **Initiative Tracker**: Track who has the initiative (Baldur's Gate)
- **Day/Night Tracker**: Toggle day/night cycle with visual indicator
- **Storm Count**: Track spells cast this turn for storm count
- **Floating Mana Reminder**: Persist mana pool display between phases with auto-clear reminder

### Game Flow
- **Phase Tracker**: Visual indicator of current game phase (Untap → Upkeep → Draw → Main 1 → Combat → Main 2 → End)
- **Priority Indicator**: Show whose priority it is during the stack
- **Quick Life Shortcuts**: Common life changes as buttons (+1, -1, +2, -2, +5, -5, half life, set to specific number)
- **Life Change Animation**: Animate life total changes with color (green for gain, red for loss) and number floating up/down
- **Undo Stack with Preview**: Show what the undo will revert before pressing it
- **Game Timer per Player**: Individual timers (not just chess clock) — track total think time per player
- **Auto-Save Game State**: Periodically auto-save so accidental app close doesn't lose the game

### Commander Damage Improvements
- **Commander Damage Matrix**: Clear grid showing all commander damage relationships between all players
- **Lethal Warning**: Flash warning when a player reaches 21 commander damage from one source
- **Commander Damage History**: Log of all commander damage dealt, not just totals

## Medium Priority

### Social & Multiplayer
- **Network Multiplayer**: Connect multiple phones/devices to the same game session via WiFi/Bluetooth
- **Spectator Mode**: Read-only view of a game in progress for observers
- **Remote Game Support**: Share game state for webcam/spelltable games
- **Player Rankings / ELO**: Track player skill ratings across multiple game sessions
- **Tournament Mode**: Run mini-tournaments (Swiss pairings, bracket) within the app
- **League Support**: Track ongoing league standings across multiple weeks

### UI/UX
- **Landscape Mode**: Optimized layout for landscape phone orientation
- **Fullscreen Mode**: Hide all browser chrome for a clean life counter experience
- **Customizable Layout**: Let players choose portrait/landscape, counter positions, font sizes
- **Theme per Player**: Each player picks their own color scheme or background
- **Commander Art Background**: Auto-fetch and display commander art as player background
- **Sound Effects**: Optional sounds for life changes, dice rolls, turn changes
- **Haptic Feedback**: Vibrate on life total changes (mobile)
- **Large Number Mode**: Extra-large life totals for visibility across the table

### Tracking & Stats
- **Per-Game Statistics**: End-of-game summary showing life total graphs, damage dealt, turns taken
- **Damage Dealt Tracking**: Track total damage each player dealt (not just life changes)
- **Comeback Tracker**: Detect and highlight comeback victories (was at lowest life, then won)
- **Fastest Win Tracker**: Track shortest game by turns and time
- **Most Played Format**: Show which format you play most often
- **Win Streak Tracking**: Track current and longest win streaks per player
- **Head-to-Head Records**: See your win rate against specific opponents

## Lower Priority

- **Voice Commands**: "Hey MTG, set player 1 to 30 life" — voice-controlled life changes
- **Planechase Support**: Random plane cards with walk-away button and planar die roller
- **Archenemy Support**: Scheme deck integration for Archenemy format
- **Two-Headed Giant Mode**: Shared life total for team formats
- **Vanguard Support**: Starting life modifiers for Vanguard format
- **Custom Game Modes**: Define custom starting life, custom counters, custom win conditions
- **Ambient Music**: Optional background music during games (lo-fi, fantasy themes)
- **Game Replay**: Step through a completed game turn by turn to review decisions
- **Achievement System**: Unlock achievements during games ("Dealt 40 damage in one turn", "Won at 1 life")
- **Dice Statistics**: Track dice roll history and show probability distributions
- **Coin Flip Tracker**: Track coin flip results for cards that require them
- **Token Army Counter**: Quick counter for large token armies (20 goblin tokens, etc.)
