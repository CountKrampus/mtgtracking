# Analytics & Insights Ideas

## High Priority

### Collection Analytics Dashboard
- **Collection Value Over Time**: Line chart tracking total collection value daily/weekly/monthly (store snapshots)
- **Value Distribution Chart**: Histogram showing how many cards fall into price ranges ($0-1, $1-5, $5-20, $20-50, $50+)
- **Top 10 Most Valuable Cards**: Leaderboard of your most expensive cards with images
- **Top 10 Most Owned Cards**: Cards you have the most copies of
- **Color Distribution Pie Chart**: Visual breakdown of your collection by color (WUBRG + colorless + multicolor)
- **Type Distribution Bar Chart**: How many creatures vs instants vs sorceries vs lands etc.
- **Rarity Breakdown**: Pie chart of Common vs Uncommon vs Rare vs Mythic
- **Set Distribution**: Bar chart showing how many cards you own per set
- **Condition Overview**: Breakdown of NM vs LP vs MP vs HP vs DMG cards
- **Average Card Value**: Average price per card across the collection

### Price Tracking
- **Individual Card Price History**: Store price snapshots over time; display as line graph per card
- **Price Change Alerts**: Highlight cards that increased or decreased in price since last update (with percentage)
- **Biggest Gainers/Losers**: Weekly report of cards with biggest price swings in your collection
- **Price Volatility Indicator**: Flag cards with historically unstable prices
- **Portfolio Performance**: Track total gain/loss if you record purchase prices vs current value

### Deck Analytics
- **Win Rate per Deck**: Track and display win rates with confidence intervals
- **Best/Worst Matchups**: Which decks or players each deck performs best/worst against
- **Deck Value Tracking**: Track each deck's value over time as card prices change
- **Most Played Deck**: Track which deck gets played the most
- **Deck Evolution Timeline**: Show how a deck changed over time (cards added/removed per version)

## Medium Priority

### Smart Insights
- **Collection Health Score**: Overall score based on diversity, condition, value distribution, and organization
- **Undervalued Cards**: Cards in your collection that are underpriced relative to play rate (sleeper picks)
- **Format Staples You Own**: Show which format staples you already have (Modern staples, Pioneer staples, etc.)
- **Deck Building Potential**: "With your collection, you could build these deck archetypes" based on staples owned
- **Missing Staples**: "You're missing these commonly played cards" based on formats you play
- **Color Affinity**: "You seem to prefer Blue/Black" based on collection composition
- **Spending Patterns**: If purchase history is tracked, show monthly spending trends
- **Set Completion Insights**: "You're 3 cards away from completing Core Set 2021" — low-hanging fruit completions

### Visual Reports
- **Monthly Collection Report**: Auto-generated summary of cards added, value changes, and activity
- **Printable Collection Summary**: One-page PDF overview for insurance or reference
- **Infographic Generator**: Create shareable infographics about your collection stats
- **Heat Map**: Calendar heat map showing collection activity (cards added per day, like GitHub contributions)
- **Mana Curve of Entire Collection**: Overall mana curve across all owned cards
- **Sunburst Chart**: Hierarchical view — Color > Type > Subtype > Individual cards

### Game Statistics
- **Player Performance Dashboard**: Per-player stats across all games (win rate, favorite format, average game length)
- **Meta Analysis**: Which colors/strategies win most in your playgroup
- **Time-Based Trends**: Do certain players win more often on certain days/times?
- **Deck Power vs Win Rate**: Scatter plot comparing deck power level to actual win rate
- **Commander Popularity**: Most played commanders in your group

## Lower Priority

- **Predicted Collection Value**: ML-based price prediction for your collection's future value
- **Tax Reporting**: Generate reports for taxable card sales (capital gains on high-value sales)
- **Insurance Valuation Report**: Formatted report suitable for insurance claims
- **Environmental Impact**: Fun stat — "Your collection weighs X lbs and would stack Y feet tall"
- **Rarity Score**: Custom score based on how rare/unique your collection is compared to average players
- **"You Should Sell" Suggestions**: Flag cards at all-time highs that you don't use in any deck
- **Weekly Email Digest**: Email summary of collection changes, price alerts, and new recommendations
- **Comparative Stats**: "Your collection is larger/more valuable than X% of MTG Tracker users" (if multi-user)
