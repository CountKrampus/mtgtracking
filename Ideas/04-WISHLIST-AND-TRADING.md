# Wishlist & Trading Ideas

## High Priority

### Wishlist Enhancements
- **Wishlist Price Alerts**: Notify when a wishlist card drops below target price (browser notification or email)
- **Wishlist Auto-Prioritize**: Auto-sort by best deals (biggest gap between current and target price)
- **Wishlist Total Budget**: Show total cost to acquire all wishlist items; filter by budget
- **Wishlist Import**: Import wishlist from text file, Moxfield, or other sources
- **Wishlist Export**: Export wishlist as text list for buying at stores
- **Wishlist Categories**: Organize wishlist items by deck they're intended for or by purpose (staples, upgrades, fun)
- **"Needed for Decks" Auto-Wishlist**: Automatically add cards to wishlist that are in decks but not in collection
- **Price Comparison**: Compare prices across multiple sources (Exor Games, Scryfall/TCGPlayer, CardKingdom)

### Trading Features
- **Trade Binder View**: Dedicated view showing cards marked as "For Trade" with values
- **Trade Value Calculator**: Select cards from both sides to calculate fair trades based on current market prices
- **Trade History**: Log completed trades with date, person, and cards exchanged
- **Trade Balance**: Track if trades were fair over time (running trade value balance)
- **Want/Have Lists**: Classic want list + have list format for sharing with trade partners
- **Trade Partner Profiles**: Save profiles for regular trade partners with notes and trade history

## Medium Priority

### Smart Shopping
- **Shopping Cart**: Build a cart of cards to buy with total cost and suggested stores
- **Store Price Comparison**: Compare prices across Exor Games, Face to Face, 401 Games, and other Canadian retailers
- **Buylist Integration**: Show what stores are buying and at what price for cards you want to sell
- **Bulk Buy Optimizer**: Given a list of cards to buy, find the cheapest combination across stores (minimize shipping)
- **Price History Graphs**: Show price trends over time for individual cards (30 day, 90 day, 1 year)
- **Market Movers**: Show cards with biggest price increases/decreases in the past week
- **Reprint Alert**: Flag wishlist cards that are getting reprinted soon (price likely to drop)

### Selling Tools
- **Sell List Generator**: Generate a formatted sell/trade list from selected cards with prices
- **Buylist Comparison**: Compare buylist prices across stores for cards you want to sell
- **Profit/Loss Tracker**: Compare purchase price vs current value to see gains/losses per card
- **TCGPlayer CSV Export**: Export in TCGPlayer-compatible format for listing cards for sale
- **Condition-Based Pricing**: Adjust prices based on card condition (NM = 100%, LP = 85%, MP = 70%, etc.)

## Lower Priority

- **eBay Sold Prices**: Pull recent eBay sold prices for more accurate market data
- **Wants Matching with Friends**: Share want/have lists with friends to find mutual trades automatically
- **Local Store Inventory Check**: If stores have APIs, check if they have wishlist cards in stock
- **Price Drop Calendar**: Track when sets rotate or get reprinted for buying opportunities
- **Speculator Mode**: Track cards you're holding for price increases with buy-in price and current value
- **Trade Binder QR Code**: Generate QR code linking to your trade binder for scanning at events
- **Bundle Deal Finder**: Find bundle deals or lots that contain multiple wishlist cards
