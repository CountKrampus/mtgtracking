# Social & Community Ideas

## High Priority

### Playgroup Features
- **Playgroup System**: Create a playgroup and invite friends by code/link
- **Shared Collection View**: See what cards your playgroup members own (opt-in)
- **Borrowing System**: Request to borrow cards from playgroup members; track who has what
- **Group Wishlist**: Shared wishlist for cards the group wants to split or group-buy
- **Game Night Scheduler**: Plan game sessions with RSVPs, time, location, and format
- **Playgroup Stats**: Aggregate win rates, most played formats, and meta trends for the group
- **Trade Within Group**: See overlap between your want list and group members' trade lists

### Sharing
- **Public Collection Profile**: Optional public page showing your collection stats and trade list
- **Share Deck Lists**: Generate shareable links for individual decks
- **Share Game Results**: Post game results to the playgroup feed
- **QR Code Business Card**: Generate a personal QR code with your trade binder link for events

## Medium Priority

### Communication
- **In-App Chat**: Simple messaging between playgroup members
- **Card Discussion**: Comment on specific cards ("This is great in my Atraxa deck")
- **Deck Feedback**: Share decks for feedback from playgroup; inline comments
- **Game Night Recap**: Post-game summary shared with the group
- **LFG (Looking for Game)**: Post when you're looking for players at local stores

### Community Features
- **Card Reviews / Ratings**: Rate cards based on your experience playing them
- **Deck Ratings**: Rate other people's shared decks
- **Collection Showcase**: Featured collections or "Collection of the Week"
- **Deck Building Challenges**: Weekly challenge ("Build a deck under $25", "Mono-green creatures only")
- **Card Recommendations Feed**: "Players with similar collections also own these cards..."
- **Meta Reports**: Community-contributed meta reports from local game stores

### Event Support
- **Event Tracker**: Log events attended (FNM, prereleases, GPs, commander nights)
- **Event Calendar**: Integration with local game store event calendars
- **Draft Tracker**: Log draft picks and results
- **Prerelease Kit Tracker**: Log sealed pool contents and record
- **Store Finder**: Find local game stores with MTG events near you (via Google Maps API)

## Lower Priority

- **Discord Bot Integration**: Bot that queries your collection from Discord ("/have Lightning Bolt?")
- **Twitter/X Share**: Share collection milestones to social media
- **Twitch Integration**: Display collection stats or life counter as Twitch overlay
- **Reddit Community Link**: Link to r/magicTCG threads about cards in your collection
- **Achievement Sharing**: Share collection milestones and achievements on social media
- **Mentorship System**: Experienced players offer deck building help to new players
- **Card Storytelling**: Attach stories to cards ("This card won me the FNM championship")
- **Photo Gallery**: Upload photos from game nights and events
